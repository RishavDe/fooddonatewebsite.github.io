About Us

This is a cumulative contribution of our team to put forward such a project that would bring a little change in our society. This is a website designed by us where anyone can donate food from their parties and organize events such as Birthdays,Anniversaries, Christmas Eve, New Year’s eve. The reason to have come up with such an idea is because often in parties we have seen that there’s a lot of food that’s wasted just because an estimated amount of guests didn’t show up. So with the help of our website we can help them donate the food to the people who haven’t got a proper meal for quite a while now. According to the recent statistics there are more than 100 crore people who sleep empty stomachs daily. This is an initiative to make a change in their lives by making a little contribution from ours. With the help of our website anyone can organize events in different kinds of NGOs for orphanages,specially abled kids,old age homes, or any such community of our society. Our website has a list of associated NGOs from which they may select the NGO of their choice and then fill a form about the details of their event such as the date,time, type of event such as a birthday, anniversary or any other occasion, then our team will come forward and organize it for them. If interested people may come and join us in our campaign by feeling out the volunteers form. We have a gallery of pictures showing the work done by us and the contributions made by everyone.

So much food is wasted in every Indian occassion. Our goal is to be a channel between this food and the people who actually need it. You can simple book a pickup of your extra food from parties, occassions etc on our Donate Page and we will schedule a pickup and you will recieve a Confirmation Email from us.

Act Now! And help us fill the thousands of hungry stomachs in our city starting from your own locality.
